Continuity Philosophy

Synk Mushroom is built around continuity.

Workflow continuity means analysis, decision, execution, response, and review should remain connected. Unnecessary interface switching weakens the flow.

Cognitive continuity means decisions are made inside attention. If the environment collapses at execution, part of the decision context is lost. Synk Mushroom protects attention from avoidable interface jumps.

Execution continuity means TradingView remains the decision surface and MT5 remains the execution terminal. Synk Mushroom exists between them as an execution route, not as an automated strategy.

Local continuity means the route stays local. No public webhook endpoint is created for order commands. The user’s own terminal and local environment remain the operating base.

Evidence continuity means execution should leave a trace. Synk Evidence Console exists because timing, response, and local route behavior should be observable. Evidence does not guarantee outcomes. Evidence clarifies responsibility boundaries.

Human discretion continuity means the human still presses. The system does not decide. The system does not advise. The system does not promise. The final action belongs to the trader.

The ideal infrastructure is quiet and supports the workflow without becoming the center of attention.

Continuity is not automation. It is the preservation of the path between attention, decision, action, response, and evidence.
