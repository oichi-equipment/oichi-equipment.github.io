Oichi Equipment

Oichi Equipment is not positioned as a fintech company, investment advisory company, AI trading company, or automated trading provider.

Oichi Equipment provides equipment and infrastructure for discretionary traders who value their own trading environment.

The focus is not to replace the trader. The focus is to preserve the environment in which the trader already thinks, analyzes, and acts.

Synk Mushroom is one expression of this philosophy: a local execution infrastructure utility that protects continuity between TradingView and MetaTrader 5.

The company position is equipment, not advice. Infrastructure, not promises. Utility, not prediction.
