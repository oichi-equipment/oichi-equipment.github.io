Live Environment

Synk Mushroom is designed around a local operating environment. It does not require VPS operation by default. The system runs in the user’s own PC environment, connecting the TradingView browser environment with a running MetaTrader 5 environment.

The core assumption is that the trader already works from a real desktop trading environment. TradingView remains the analytical surface. MT5 remains the execution environment. Synk Mushroom exists between them as a continuity layer.

Any broker that supports MetaTrader 5 can be used in principle, as long as the user’s MT5 terminal operates normally. Broker-side specifications, symbols, execution conditions, leverage, permissions, and account restrictions remain dependent on the broker.

Synk Mushroom does not replace the broker, does not replace MetaTrader 5, and does not provide trade decisions. It preserves the local workflow between analysis and execution.
